# Contributors

Many people have contributed translations to the iNaturalist Rails app over the years, mostly through the crowdsourcing platforms [Translatewiki](https://translatewiki.net/w/i.php?title=Special:Translate&group=out-inaturalist) (January 2015 - September 2019) and [Crowdin](https://crowdin.com/project/inaturalistweb) (September 2019 - present). Thank you to everyone who has helped translate iNaturalist. You've allowed iNat serve a much broader audience through your work.
